Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweeet caramelized onions,tart
cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3//4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus moe for pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the ocaccia dough
